Free Weights and Machines.

Free Weights.

1. Versatility: Free weights are versatile and can be used for a wide range of exercises.

2. Functional Strength: They help develop functional strength because they engage stabilizing muscles and require balance and coordination.

3. Progression: It's easier to progress with free weights, as you can increase the weight in smaller increments.

4. Cost and Space Efficient: They are generally cheaper and take up less space than machines, making them a good choice for home gyms.

Gym Machines.

1. Ease of Use: Gym machines are generally easier to use because they guide your movement and you only need to adjust the weight and seat height.

2. Safety: They are safer for beginners because the risk of dropping weights or losing control is minimized.

3. Isolation of Muscles: Machines allow you to isolate specific muscles, which can be beneficial for targeted training or rehabilitation.

4. Stability: They provide stability which can be helpful for those with balance issues or for elderly individuals.
